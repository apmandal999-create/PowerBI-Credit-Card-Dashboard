 🔍 Key Insights

- **Total Revenue: $55.32M** across 656K transactions with $7.84M in interest earned
- **Blue card holders dominate** — contributing $46M (83%) of total revenue despite being the base tier
- **Graduates generate the highest revenue** at $22M, outpacing all other education segments
- **Businessmen lead by job category** at $17M, followed by white-collar workers at $10M
- **Swipe transactions account for $35M** (63%) of revenue vs Chip ($17M) and Online ($3M)
- **Male customers (40-50 age group)** are the highest revenue segment at $14M
- **High-income group males** generate $22M vs $7M for females — a 3x gap worth investigating
- **Bills ($14M) and Entertainment ($10M)** are the top spending categories by expense type
